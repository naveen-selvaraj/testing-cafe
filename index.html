Our Story

M. BHUVANESH RAJ

Founder Of Mag's Cafe

"My friend suggested many business ideas for starting a startup, but we was drawn only to the
food industry, specifically cafes and restaurants. After considering various franchise options, We
had an epiphany: why not create my own brand? To prepare for this, we decided to gain practical
experience in cooking different dishes. With time, we honed my skills and developed numerous
ideas for unique food offerings. Finally, feeling confident and well-prepared, we decided to take
the plunge and open our own cafe. In honor of my father, we named it 'MAG'S Cafe' after him,
with ('Magendran Singaram') as a tribute to our family. Opening this cafe has been a dream for
seven years, and now it's finally coming true. and we excited to embark on this journey, armed with
knowledge and passion for food."
